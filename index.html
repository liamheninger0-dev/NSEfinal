Our Story

A New Standard for Excellence

New Standard Elite is a premier distance running group representing Brigham Young University. Founded on the belief that greatness is forged through relentless consistency, community, and competition, NSE is home to some of the most competitive middle- and long-distance runners in the NCAA.

We train together, compete together, and push each other beyond conventional limits. The name says it all — we are not here to meet the standard. We are here to set it.
